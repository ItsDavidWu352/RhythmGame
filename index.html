    • How to Play

    Hit the falling notes at the sound of the music's beat. Use the sticks and hit the left and right drums as the notes fall down on the screen.

    Press the play button to show the music list. Hover over a thumbnail to preview the music choice.

    Drum Controls: Left drum controls the left side and the right drum controls the right side.

    Keyboard Controls: Hit the note on the left using the S key, and hit the note on the right using the D key.

    Let's start playing!

    Setting Instructions

    • Speed: How fast you want the notes to drop from above
    • Complication: Sets a challenge during gameplay
    • MV: Turns on a Motion Video on selected eligible songs
    • Randomize: Randomizes note placement
